    📖 How to Play
    1
    Four Progressive LevelsComplete Level 1 to unlock Level 2, then Levels 3 and 4. You cannot skip ahead — finish each level before the next one opens. Progress saves automatically; use Resume on the level screen to continue.
    2
    Level 1 — Organ FunctionsRead one organ function at a time and click the matching organ on the body map. Correct picks add +4 points and build your streak; wrong picks cost −1 point, reset your streak to 0, and make all organs unidentified again. Reach a 10-streak and identify all 10 organs within 30 attempts to unlock Level 2.
    3
    Level 2 — Disease MatchingSame body-map format: read a disease name and click the organ it affects. Same streak rules as Level 1 (+4 correct, −1 wrong, 10-streak pass, 30 attempts).
    4
    Level 3 — Food MatchingSame body-map format: read a healing food with its hint, then click the organ that benefits most. Same streak rules as Level 1.
    5
    Level 4 — Symptom MatchingSame body-map format: read a patient symptom and click the affected organ. Same streak rules as Level 1. Finish all four levels to complete the game.
    6
    Finish & Track ProgressAfter Level 4 you see your results and total score. My Progress shows your own level scores so you can review and improve — there is no competition with others.
